Top Tracks

Privacy Policy

By choosing to use this app, you agree to the use of your Spotify account's top tracks, and your username.

How your data is used

This app uses the Spotify Web API to retrieve your account's top tracks and your display name.
Your data is then used to generate a cool image.

How your data is stored

This app does not save any of your data.
Your data is only used to generate the image displayed to you.


All your data is not saved nor shared.
All Spotify data that is retrieved only gets sent to you, nothing is stored on the server.
No data can be retrieved without your log-in.

You can remove access to this and other apps by going to your Spotify Account Settings and clicking "Remove Access" for TopTracks.
